2023

July

  • Invited by Gioele Zardini, I gave a presentation at the Institute for Dynamic Systems and Control at ETH Zurich, Switzerland.

June

  • Invited by Matt Harris, I gave a presentation at the MAE/ECE Colloquium at Utah State University, Logan, UT.
  • We presented our paper “Inverse Matrix Games With Unique Quantal Response Equilibrium”, “Online Poisoning Attacks Against Data-Driven Predictive Control”, and “Cost Design in Atomic Routing Games” at 2023 American Control Conference, San Diego, CA.

April

  • We submitted our paper “Soft-Bellman Equilibrium in Affine Markov Games: Forward Solutions and Inverse Learning” to 2023 IEEE Conference on Decision and Control. This paper extends our previous results on inverse games from static games to Markov dynamic games.

  • Our paper “Vertiport Selection in Hybrid Air-Ground Transportation Networks via Mathematical Programs with Equilibrium Constraints” has been published in IEEE Transactions on Control of Network Systems.
  • Invited by Michael Amitay, I gave a presentatuion to the Mechanical, Aerospace, and Nuclear Engineering Department at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY.
  • Our paper “Real-Time Sequential Conic Optimization for Multi-Phase Rocket Landing Guidance” and “Dynamic Routing in Stochastic Urban Air Mobility Networks: A Markov Decision Process Approach” has been accepted for publication in 2023 IFAC World Congress.

  • Invited by Jeff Scruggs, I gave a presentation to the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI.
  • Our paper “Real-Time Quadrotor Trajectory Optimization with Time-Triggered Corridor Constraints” has been published in the Journal of Guidance, Control, and Dynamics.
